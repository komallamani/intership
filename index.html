Namma Karunadu


"Namma Karunadu"(Our Land of Karunadu) is an affectionate term for the Indian state of Karnataka, meaning "our land of black soil" or "lofty land," referring to its Deccan Plateau location.

It is a vibrant southern state known for its rich culture,
Kannada language, scenic Western Ghats, ancient history, and bustling tech hub, Bengaluru.
